Frequently Asked Questions

It says I don’t have a working Internet connection, while I’m sure I do.

your phone rooted? This is a common issue for rooted phones.It’s not something we can fix, we have tried to fix it in the past but it’s something external and is only a problem with certain rooted phones.

Swiping the calendar / tapping a day is way too sensitive!

This seems to be the case on some devices only. We are not sure how and why it happens but rest assured that we are working on it.

I added a pinlock but now I can’t remove it!

Go to your profile page and tap the little red pencil next to “Your Lockscreen Pincode”. In the popup tap “Remove Pinlock”.

Why do I sometimes see a notification while I haven’t set any notifications?

We sometimes remind you to use App, but you can disable this by going to Settings.
